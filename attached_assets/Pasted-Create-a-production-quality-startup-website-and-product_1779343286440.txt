Create a production-quality startup website and product experience for a venture-scale Indian startup called “LocalGrid”.

CORE IDEA
LocalGrid is a hyperlocal economic platform for India’s informal workers, service providers, freelancers, and small sellers.
It helps anyone:
- create a digital identity
- get discovered locally
- show a professional portfolio made by the platform
- receive inquiries and bookings
- manage work and orders
- sell products through a mini-store
- build trust through reviews, verification, and activity history

IMPORTANT PRODUCT POSITIONING
Do NOT position this as just:
- a local marketplace
- a classifieds app
- a simple ecommerce site
- a freelancer listing site

Position it as:
“economic participation infrastructure for local workers and micro-businesses.”

TARGET USERS
1. Service providers:
- electricians
- plumbers
- tutors
- beauticians
- mehendi artists
- tailors
- carpenters
- repair workers
- photographers
- freelancers
- home-based professionals

2. Small sellers:
- homemade food businesses
- clothing resellers
- artisans
- handicraft sellers
- local brands
- rural product sellers
- small merchants

3. Demand side users:
- customers looking for trusted local services
- customers looking for nearby products and sellers

MOST IMPORTANT REQUIREMENT
Every service provider must get a beautiful portfolio page generated and styled by LocalGrid itself.
The worker should not need design knowledge.
The website must clearly show that LocalGrid automatically creates:
- profile
- portfolio
- service cards
- work gallery
- reviews section
- trust badges
- booking/contact modules

This portfolio should feel premium, modern, and mobile-first.
Think of it as:
“your business profile + mini portfolio website + local visibility page”
all created automatically inside LocalGrid.

WEBSITE GOAL
Build a complete startup website that looks investor-ready, premium, minimal, modern, and trustworthy.
It should be good enough to present to:
- early users
- angel investors
- seed funds
- strategic partners

- minimalistic

Animation must be:

BRAND STORY
LocalGrid is building the digital identity and operating layer for India’s local economy.
It turns informal work into discoverable, trusted, structured digital business presence.

MAIN MESSAGES
The website must communicate these clearly:
- Start your service business online in minutes
- Get your own professional portfolio automatically
- Be discovered by nearby customers
- Manage bookings, orders, and work history
- Sell products and services in one place
- Build trust through verification, ratings, and proof of work
- Grow locally first, then scale city by city

PRIMARY CTA
“Join the waitlist”
Secondary CTA:
“See how LocalGrid works”
